How to Use This Website

This website aims to educate users about the progress of the coronavirus pandemic. It alerts the user on the safety level of the destination in NSW they are planning to visit.

This website will achieve these goals by presenting data in a visual, minimalistic interface and allow users to make easy, informed decisions about the places they are travelling so they may keep themselves and the ones they love safe.

Click on the search icon in the map to search for a location. Once a location has been searched, a safety level indicator as well as some general safety tips/suggestions will appear at the bottom. At the top, the current date, daily count of cases and cumulative cases and deaths in NSW are displayed. Users can also choose to view a graph in 'detail mode'. Additional features include personalised twitter feeds, a share button and a mode toggle to switch between 'simple' and 'detail' modes.
