Activities

All Our Activities

Ride off into the rocky, breathtaking crimson views of Ulaan Khadny Khondii, a reserved area of grassland especially for horseback riding! This is a once in a lifetime opportunity!

Practice your archery skills at our archery range! For extra awesomeness, pay an additional $10 for our horseback archery tutorial and compete with your friends for a limited time of 20 minutes.

This is the hunting tour. You will be taken to point Saikhan on lake Taivan where you will disperse in groups of 3. You will catch and skin a wild goat using our provided standard wooden bow and arrow. This activity will take about 2 hours to complete. Ask reception for further details.

Since 2015, we own a 1 acre cultural games arena. You will view tight horse races, brutal nadaam boxing matches and archery competitions. Highly recommended to visit this place at least once during your stay!

Our 4 star rated restaurant will leave your tastebuds longing for more and a warm, fuzzy feeling you will NOT get anywhere else (see "Dining" to download our restaurant menu)!

We have included a standard food tasting tour which showcases the traditional culinary practices of the ancient Mongol ancestors. Highly recommended for all cultural foodies out there!

This is the "Khurim Ger" in which we celebrate weddings and anniversaries! This giant felt house can accomodate up to 70 people inside! Want a grand party? You came to the right place (see below for further details)!

Khurim Ceremony

Here at Altan Steppe, we wish the very best to newlywed couples embarking on an amazing, life-changing journey together. And to do that, we have spared only the finest 'khurim ger' in which we conduct our traditional all-day wedding ceremonies, complete with a great 'wedding barbecue', traditional folk songs and free gifts. Start off your marriage on a grand note! Ask us for details at the reception! A valid discount quiz promo will give you 20% off!
