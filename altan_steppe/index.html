Welcome!

'Sain Bainuu' to all lovers of adventure, history and the pure, raw outdoors. If you want to experience the untamed awesomeness of Khaan era Mongolia, then this is the place for you! Guests will stay in a traditional 'ger' for the entire stay amidst a vast stretch of grasslands just 15 minutes drive from Thredbo. With loads of fun activities and a free spirited, chill (literally) atmosphere, you'll never want to leave!

How To Get There

Reachable in 2 hours from Canberra Airport, the Eagles' Nest Restaurant is our reception point. From here, a 14 minute journey in our specialised 4WD cars will carry you directly to your very own, fully furnished ger. For a fuller, richer experience, book a ger in the Altan Steppe now! If planning a holiday lasting more than 3 days, it is highly recommended to book another accomodation in Thredbo; however you will be still allowed access to the resort and all its facilities/activities.
