Dining

Zurkh Be Suns

Our resort includes a 4 star rated restaurant that serves 3 course meals for lunch, breakfast and dinner. The restaurant is also open 23 hours as a souvenir shop selling various toys and instruments. We emphasise quality over quantity and always look to serve our best, cleanest and healthiest food to all our guests, but most of all, we look to leave you with a warm and nourishing feeling of satisfaction that lingers for plenty of time to come. Hunting Tour

Our hunting tour begins with a 5 minute journey to Lake Taivan. You will be dropped off at Point Saikhan to hunt a wild goat. You will be given a standard composite bow (hand-carved), arrows and a hunting eagle. Once you finish hunting, we will then demonstrate to you how to cook some mouth-watering, heavenly 'boodog' in our restaurant kitchen. You will also take part in the cooking; the end result is a rewarding, satisfying meal you'll never forget!
